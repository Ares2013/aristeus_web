About Aristeus Financial Service

As a professional Forex broker, we initially specialise in Forex and now are gradually expanding the range of products and currently our customers can choose from over 1000 CFD or Spread Betting instruments across Forex, Indices, Shares, Commodities, Financials, ETFs and Options.

With our customers’ trust, our expertise in delivering first-class trading environment and innovation at the heart of the company we look forward to expanding our reach and providing investment opportunities to retail, professional and institutional traders across the world.

Why choose us?

We see every of our clients as a valued partner rather than a number, and we’re dedicated to establishing a long-term relationship with you to help you trade successfully.

Superior service

We believe in building long-term relationships with our active traders. A personal account manager comes as standard, and our customer support team are available 24 hours a day, from Sunday to Friday. We provide our clients individual support during the whole trading journey.

Client money protection

As a regulated company, we hold all retail client funds in a fully segregated account, meaning they are not mixed with our own funds. So you can trade with the confidence that your funds are protected.

Diverse trading platforms

Backed by our powerful technology, our diverse trading platforms will meet our client’s need to trade no matter where they are. Our clients can manage trades from any device including smartphone, desktop, laptop or tablet

Licensed broker
